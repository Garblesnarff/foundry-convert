## Description

100% local. 100% offline. 100% yours.

Raw media files are ore in the wrong alloy. Foundry Convert transmutes them — batch-converting video, audio, and images across 19 format presets at the full speed of your Apple Silicon, without touching the internet.

Drop files. Pick the alloy. Let the forge run.

**Transmute any format into any other.**
- Batch conversion: Drop hundreds of files, convert them all in parallel
- 19 format presets: MP4 (H.264/H.265), WebM, MKV, MOV, GIF, MP3, AAC, FLAC, WAV, OGG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, AVIF, TIFF, and more
- Parallel processing: Up to 4 simultaneous conversions using all your cores
- Output control: Set your destination folder, naming convention, and quality level
- Progress tracking: Per-file progress bars with time remaining estimates
- Keyboard-native: ⌘O to import, ⌘↩ to start, ⌘, for settings

**The forge processes four files at once while you work on the fifth.**
Select your output format. Drag in your source files. Hit convert. Foundry Convert dispatches up to four parallel forge queues — each file transmuting independently at full GPU/CPU speed. No waiting in a single-file line. The batch is done when the batch is done.

**Built for creators, archivists, and anyone who works with media.**
The video editor converting client footage to a timeline-friendly codec. The podcast producer encoding WAV sessions to compressed MP3. The photographer batch-resizing RAW crops for web upload. The developer converting assets for a game or app. Foundry Convert is the forge that speaks every media language.
